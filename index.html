About

My name is Trent, I go by Kodiak Havoc a content creationist that mainly uses Twitch as means to connect and make content for those who enjoy my show! I have started streaming in August 2016 just on a whim to experience what it is like to put myself out in front of a crowd. I was never a fan of being center of attention but as the months went on I had grown use to public speaking and entertainment, I still have plenty to learn and grow in this large niche and I thank and love you all who stand beside me and push me to be a better content creator!!
